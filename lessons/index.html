Lesson Structure

1. Student Recap

Address student's questions and concerns. Student demonstrates progress since their previous lesson.

2. Listen to song

Learn to develop hearing and learn the song by ear. Chris will write the notes on paper for you (song transcription).

3. Learn to play

Chris demonstrates how to play the song to the student. Note by note, starting slow and gradually increasing to full speed.

4. Practice Plan

Student has played through the parts and it is time to perfect them. Questions and concerns are addressed again and a practice plan is devised.
